Sous Vide Steak Dinner

Description

Restaurant-quality eating, at home, for a fraction of the cost.

Ingredients

  • New York Strip Steak
  • One whole clove of garlic
  • Baked Potato
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • Potato fixin's

Steps (Steak)

  1. Set your sous vide to the appropriate temperature based on your preference for donenness:
    • Rare: 120°F to 128°F
    • Medium-rare: 129°F to 134°F
    • Medium: 135°F to 144°F
    • Medium-well: 145°F to 155°F
    • Well done: 156°F and above
  2. Set your pad of butter out to soften to room temperature.
  3. Give your steak an appropriate rub. I'm happy with salt, pepper, and a little garlic powder.
  4. Set your clove of garlic into the surface of the steak with gentle pressure before following the next step.
  5. Vacuum-seal your steak using
    • A vacuum sealer
      • or
    • A BPA-free zip-locking freezer bag via the 'submersion' technique.
  6. Cook for between one and two-and-a-half hours. Note: do notcook for less than one hour! Do not cook from frozen!
  7. Once your timer is up, remove the bag and place it in the freezer for around ten minutes, or the refrigerator for as long as 15 minutes. This cooling-down of the outside of the steak will prevent over-cooking the inside during the searing step, which comes next.
  8. Get a cast-iron skillet as hot as you possibly can. It can help to cook it in the stove at the stove's highest temperature before removing it and transferring it to a stovetop. Cooking on an outdoor grill would do very well here too.
  9. Once you've chilled the steak for the appropriate length of time, remove it from the refrigerator / freezer and open the bag. Remove the clove of garlic, then transfer directly to the cast-iron skillet.
  10. Sear for roughly one minute on one side.
  11. Using a set of tongs, hold the steak sideways and sear (a little downard pressure would not hurt!) on the rind to render out further fat.
  12. Flip to the final side of the steak, and place the tablespoon of butter atop it. Let that melt over the steak for 60-90 seconds and remove to a plate for serving.
